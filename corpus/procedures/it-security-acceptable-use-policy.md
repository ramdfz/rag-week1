# IT Security and Acceptable Use Policy

**Owner:** Information Technology
**Applies to:** All employees, contractors, and temporary staff with access to Meridian Health Partners systems

## 1. Purpose

This policy sets minimum expectations for the secure and appropriate use of company-owned devices, accounts, and network resources.

## 2. Account Security

2.1. Multi-factor authentication is required on all company accounts, no exceptions.
2.2. Passwords must be at least 14 characters and must not be reused across personal and company accounts.
2.3. Shared logins are prohibited. Each employee must use their own individually assigned credentials.
2.4. Suspected account compromise must be reported to IT Security within one hour of discovery.

## 3. Device Use

3.1. Company laptops must have full-disk encryption and endpoint protection software active at all times.
3.2. Personal devices accessing company email or documents must be enrolled in the mobile device management (MDM) program.
3.3. Software installation outside the approved catalog requires IT approval before installation.
3.4. Lost or stolen devices must be reported to IT within one hour so remote wipe can be initiated.

## 4. Network and Remote Access

4.1. Company systems containing sensitive data may only be accessed over the company VPN; see the Remote Access and VPN Policy for connection steps.
4.2. Public Wi-Fi use for company work requires VPN connection at all times, no exceptions.
4.3. Personal cloud storage services (e.g., personal Dropbox, personal Google Drive) may not be used to store company documents.

## 5. Acceptable Use

5.1. Company systems are provided for business use; incidental personal use is permitted if it does not interfere with work or violate other sections of this policy.
5.2. Employees may not use company systems to access, store, or transmit content that violates the Code of Conduct Policy or Anti-Harassment Policy.
5.3. Employees may not attempt to bypass security controls, including firewall rules or endpoint protection, for any reason.

## 6. Enforcement

6.1. Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination, consistent with the Code of Conduct Policy.
6.2. IT Security may temporarily suspend account access without prior notice if a security incident is suspected, pending investigation.
